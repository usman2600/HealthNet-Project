## HealthNet: Rural Patient Health Wallet  

### Project Overview  
HealthNet is an offline-first digital health wallet designed to help Community Health Workers and Primary Health Centers securely create, store, and share patient health records in rural communities.  

The platform ensures continuity of care by storing data locally and synchronizing when connectivity is restored. It enables portable records through QR code sharing and includes an AI-powered feature that translates complex medical jargon into simple language for patients and health workers.  

### Problem Statement  
- Fragmented Records: Reliance on paper-based systems leads to lost data  
- Broken Referrals: Loss of medical history during patient transfers  
- Low Health Literacy: Difficulty understanding complex medical terms  
- Financial Gaps: Lack of simple digital payment tracking in PHCs  

### The Solution  
HealthNet provides a simple, offline-first platform that enables:  
- Secure Registration: Patient history tracking from day one  
- Visit Recording: Logging symptoms, diagnoses, and prescriptions  
- QR-Based Sharing: Instant record sharing across facilities without internet  
- AI Explainer: Simplifies medical information for better patient understanding  
- Integrated Payments: Seamless healthcare service transactions
  
### Key Features  
- Patient Profile Management  
- Visit Records Logging  
- QR Code Record Sharing (Offline-capable)  
- AI Explanation Module (Powered by Lightweight LLM)  
- Payment Integration (Interswitch)  
- Offline-First Architecture with Auto-Sync  

### Tech Stack  
- Frontend: Flutter / React Native  
- Backend: Node.js / FastAPI  
- Database: SQLite / Hive (Local Storage)  
- Standards: HL7 FHIR (R4)  
- Payments: Interswitch Integration
