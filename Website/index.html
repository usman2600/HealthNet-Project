The Problem

Healthcare in Rural Areas is Broken

Millions of patients lose critical medical history every time they visit a new facility.

📄 Fragmented Records

Paper-based systems lead to lost data and incomplete patient histories.

🔗 Broken Referrals

Medical history disappears when patients are transferred between facilities.

📖 Low Health Literacy

Patients struggle to understand complex prescriptions and lab results.

💳 Financial Gaps

No simple way to track or manage healthcare payments at PHCs.
